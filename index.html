SMURF: Segmentation and Manifold UnRolling Framework¶

Welcome to SMURF!

SMURF performs soft segmentation of VisiumHD data, facilitating the creation of a cells*genes anndata object. SMURF uses high-resolution images from VisiumHD for nuclei segmentation and then assigns the transcripts recovered from each capture ‘spot’ to a nearby cell. See preprint paper for more details.
